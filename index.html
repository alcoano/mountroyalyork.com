Mount Royal-York AA Group

Welcome to the Mount Royal York Group of Alcoholics Anonymous.

We have an open meeting on Tuesdays at 6:00pm ET and a closed meeting on Thursdays at 6:00pm ET. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, we are currently meeting remotely.

Join the virtual meeting.

Instructions for Online Meeting

Our meetings are hosted at https://meeting.mountroyalyork.com using Google Meet. You will not need to install anything if you are using a computer. On a smart phone, you will be directed to install the Google Meet app if you do not already have it. The virtual meeting room will open fifteen minutes prior to the meeting's start time. You are welcome to stick around after the meeting for fellowship.

Please:

  1. Wear headphones to eliminate echo.
  2. Mute yourself when you are not talking.
